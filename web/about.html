Library Management is Free

Librarika is free for all for up to 2,000 (10,000) titles or records, however we may increase this limit for non-profit or charity organisations based on actual needs.

Who can use Library Management?

Everybody! University library, college library, school library, corporate office, public library, private library, family library or even personal library can be maintained through Librarika. The possibilities are endless.
